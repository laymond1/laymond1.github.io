**Authors:** **<u>Wonseon Lim</u>**, Yi Zhou, Dae-Won Kim, Jaesung Lee

We introduce MixER, a mixup-based experience replay technique that combines memory samples and current-task samples to alleviate catastrophic forgetting in online class-incremental learning.
